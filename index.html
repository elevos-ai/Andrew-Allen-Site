About Andrew

Experienced, direct, and trusted by families who want honest guidance.

Andrew Allen is a member of the Distinguished Sales Club, a 16-time recipient of the prestigious Circle of Excellence award, and a Presidents Producer award winner, presented to agents who pair outstanding sales production with a demonstrated commitment to high-quality customer service.

Awards and record sales numbers have never been the goal. Andrew's reputation has come from doing a job he loves and making clients his number one priority. He is known for being absolutely honest and frank, practical in his advice, and committed to getting things right the first time, from professional staging and photography to pricing and launch strategy.

Andrew is also a connector by nature. When someone mentions a problem, his mental rolodex starts turning toward the person who can solve it, whether it is real-estate related or not. That energy, combined with a straightforward service style, is why endorsements continue to show up across Facebook, Zillow, and other public review platforms.

Andrew is 49, married, and the father of one boy and one girl. He is deeply involved in school activities, the lake community where his family lives, and the outdoor lifestyle that shaped his “Lake Guy” identity, from sailing and slalom water skiing to biking, hiking, and snow skiing.
