About Andrew

All About the Lake Guy

Andrew Allen is a member of the Distinguished Sales Club, a 16-time recipient of the prestigious Circle of Excellence award, and a Presidents Producer award winner.

Awards and record sales numbers have never been the goal. Andrew's reputation has come from doing a job he loves, making clients his number one priority, and giving advice that is honest, practical, and direct.

Andrew is also a connector by nature. When someone mentions a problem, his mental rolodex starts turning toward the person who can solve it, whether it is real-estate related or not.

He is deeply involved in school activities, the lake community where his family lives, and the outdoor lifestyle that shaped his “Lake Guy” identity.
